About the park camper tour

Havelock Island is known to be the best location for scuba diving in Andaman. The coral belt surrounding the Elephant beach is home to rich marine life which is rarely found anywhere else. Since every Andaman tour package includes a visit to Havelock Island, it is an ideal place for scuba diving.
